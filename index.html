Featured Projects

VDO.Ninja

Bring remote video into OBS via WebRTC

Peer-to-peer video streaming directly in your browser. No downloads, no accounts, no data collection. Used by NASA, AWS, and Twitch for professional remote production. Supports up to 30 guests per room with ultra-low latency.

Open VDO.Ninja Documentation GitHub

SocialStream.Ninja

Consolidate live chat from 100+ platforms

Aggregate chat from YouTube, Twitch, TikTok, Kick, Facebook, and more into a single dashboard. Features chat overlays for OBS, text-to-speech, AI chatbots, auto-responders, and cross-platform message relaying. Completely free.

About

I'm Steve Seguin, a software developer focused on video streaming and live production tools. What started as OBS.Ninja in 2018 has grown into an ecosystem of projects used by over a million creators, broadcasters, and organizations worldwide.

Most of my projects are free and require no accounts. They're hobby projects built to solve real problems for streamers and content creators.

Ninjafy Tools is my registered business (a trade name of Skindeep.AI Inc., Ontario, Canada). NinjaBacker launched in December 2025 with zero commissions. NinjaChatter and Meshcast 2.0 are coming in early 2026.
